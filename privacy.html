Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 9, 2025

At Generous Corp, we value privacy by design. Solar Energy Powerflow Monitor is built to operate without collecting, storing, or tracking personal data.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect names, email addresses, or usage behavior. There is no analytics, crash reporting, or third-party ad tracking.

2. App Functionality

To operate, the App may connect to devices on your local network in order to:

  • Access your MonitorMySolar dongle to retrieve configuration details used for setting up your MQTT broker (via the dongle details endpoint).
  • Connect to your MQTT broker to read real-time solar inverter data from your MonitorMySolar Universal Solar Inverter Dongle.
  • Communicate with your EVCC server (optional). The App may check whether an EVCC server is reachable on your local network in order to support EV charging management via EVCC.

All connections are user-configured and connect directly to your own servers. We do not operate or have access to these servers.

3. Permissions

The App requires network access to connect to your MQTT broker and optionally your EVCC server. The App does not request access to Location, Contacts, Files, Photos, Microphone, or Camera.

4. Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Connection profiles: MQTT host, port, username, and dongle identifier
  • Passwords: MQTT credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain
  • Preferences: Theme settings and display preferences stored in UserDefaults
  • iCloud sync: If iCloud is enabled on your device, the App will automatically sync and back up your settings using iCloud Key-Value Store.

This data remains on your device (and iCloud account if enabled) and is not transmitted to Generous Corp.
